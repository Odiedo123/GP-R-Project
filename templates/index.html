The Problem Of Western Media

Is African representation in Western media is biased? How many times have we heard of Wars? Poverty? What about Technology? Why does the last one stand out? Why does media focus on the negative and what are its devasting effects on the African Population?

Tech In Africa

Africa's booming tech sector is a multibillion dollar industry but is shuned by global news outlets giving rise to stereotypes and biases such as the "dark" nation, "primitive" continent and that Africa is a "lost cause". Estimates say Africa loses around £3.2bn yearly in inflated interest payments due to persistent negative stereotypes.
